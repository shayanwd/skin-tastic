The Marvelous

World of AHA

AHA, or Alpha Hydroxy Acid, is a type of skin peel that primarily targets the skin’s surface. It’s perfect for tackling issues like fine lines, uneven skin tone, and mild pigmentation. AHA exfoliates the topmost layer of your skin, leaving it smoother and more radiant.

BHA: The Solution for

Acne-Prone Skin

Are pesky acne breakouts causing you endless frustration? BHA, or Beta Hydroxy Acid, might be your skincare superhero. BHA penetrates deeper into your pores, making it highly effective in treating acne and clogged pores. Say goodbye to those stubborn blemishes!

TCA: The Deeper Dive

into Skin Rejuvenation

If you’re seeking more intensive skin rejuvenation, TCA, or Trichloroacetic Acid, is the way to go. TCA peels can address deep wrinkles, sun damage, and severe pigmentation issues. This peel goes beyond the surface to unveil a brand-new you.

FAQs

Q: What Does a Skin Peel Feel Like?

A: A skin peel typically feels like a tingling or warm sensation on your skin during the procedure. Post-peel, you may experience some redness and mild discomfort, which are temporary.

Q: How Many Sessions Do I Need for Optimal Results?

A: The number of sessions required varies based on your skin’s condition and your desired results. Our experts will customize a treatment plan just for you.

Q: Is There Downtime After a Skin Peel?

A: Yes, there is some downtime after a skin peel, depending on the type and depth of the peel. It’s essential to follow post-peel instructions diligently to ensure a smooth recovery.

Q: Can I Wear Makeup After a Skin Peel?

A: We recommend avoiding makeup immediately after a peel to allow your skin to heal. Once the peeling process is complete, you can gradually reintroduce makeup.

Q: Are Skin Peels Expensive?
