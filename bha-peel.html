BHA Peel

A BHA Peel is a cosmetic procedure that uses Beta Hydroxy Acid, specifically salicylic acid, to exfoliate the outer layer of your skin. This process removes dead skin cells, unclogs pores, and stimulates the production of new, healthier skin cells. The result? Smoother, clearer, and more youthful-looking skin.

BHA (Beta Hydroxy Acid) peel

at a Glance

Procedure Time: The actual application of the BHA peel typically takes around 15 to 30 minutes, although the entire appointment may last longer due to preparation and aftercare instructions.

Number of Treatments: The number of BHA peel treatments needed can vary depending on the individual’s skin condition and goals. In some cases, a single treatment may provide noticeable results, while others may require a series of treatments spaced several weeks apart for optimal results. A dermatologist or skincare professional can provide guidance on the recommended number of sessions.

Duration of Results: The results of a BHA peel can last for several weeks to a few months, depending on factors such as the individual’s skin type, the depth of the peel, and their skincare routine. To maintain and extend the results, regular skincare maintenance and follow-up treatments may be recommended.

Procedure

Consultation: Your skincare specialist will assess your skin type and discuss your goals to determine the most suitable treatment plan.

Preparation: Your skin will be cleansed and prepped for the peel.

Application: The BHA solution is applied to your skin. You may experience a mild tingling sensation.

Duration: The peel typically takes around 20-30 minutes.

Neutralization: The BHA solution is neutralized, and your skin is cleansed.

Aftercare: You’ll receive post-peel care instructions to ensure optimal results and minimal downtime.

Benefits

A BHA Peel offers a range of benefits for your skin, including:

Exfoliation: Removal of dead skin cells reveals fresher, younger-looking skin.

Acne Treatment: BHA Peels are effective in treating acne and reducing the appearance of acne scars.

Unclogged Pores: The peel clears out pores, reducing the risk of blackheads and whiteheads.

Improved Texture: Skin becomes smoother and more even in tone.

Youthful Radiance: Stimulated collagen production leads to a youthful glow.

Aftercare

Following your BHA Peel at Skintastic, it’s crucial to follow the recommended aftercare routine. This may include:

Sun Protection: Protect your newly revealed skin by wearing sunscreen daily.

Moisturizing: Keep your skin hydrated to support the healing process.

Avoiding Harsh Products: Steer clear of harsh skincare products for a few days post-peel.

Minimizing Sun Exposure: Limit your sun exposure, especially during peak hours.

Is It Safe?

Yes, when performed by trained professionals, a BHA Peel is generally safe. The procedure is well-tolerated by most individuals and has minimal side effects. Your Skintastic technician will discuss any potential risks during your consultation.

How Long Do

Results Last?

The longevity of results can vary from person to person. Generally, you can expect to enjoy the benefits of a BHA Peel for several months. However, to maintain the effects, periodic touch-up treatments are recommended.

FAQs

Q1: Is a BHA Peel painful?

A1: Most clients report minimal discomfort, often described as a mild tingling or warmth during the procedure.

Q2: How many sessions are needed for optimal results?

A2: The number of sessions required can vary, but many individuals see significant improvements after just one session. Your skincare specialist will advise you based on your skin’s needs.

Q3: Can I wear makeup after a BHA Peel?

A3: It’s recommended to avoid makeup for the first 24 hours post-peel. After that, you can gradually reintroduce makeup products.

Q4: Is there downtime after a BHA Peel?

A4: While there may be some redness and peeling, downtime is generally minimal, and you can resume your daily activities.

Q5: Are BHA Peels suitable for all skin types?

A5: BHA Peels are effective for various skin types, but your skincare specialist will determine the suitability during your consultation.
