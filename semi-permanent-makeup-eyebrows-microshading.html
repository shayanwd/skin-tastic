Eyebrows Microshading

at a Glance

Procedure Time:

The procedure time for Semi-Permanent Eyebrow Microshading typically ranges from 1.5 to 2.5 hours, although it can vary depending on individual factors and the specific technique used by the technician.

Number of Treatments:

Achieving the desired results with Semi-Permanent Eyebrow Microshading often requires more than one session. In most cases, clients will need two sessions:

Initial Session: During the first appointment, the technician will create the initial shape and fill in the eyebrows with micro shading. This sets the foundation for the final look.

Touch-up Session: Approximately 4 to 6 weeks after the initial session, a follow-up appointment, known as a touch-up session, is required. This allows the technician to make any necessary adjustments, deepen the color, and ensure the longevity of the results.

Duration of Results: The duration of results from Semi-Permanent Eyebrow Microshading can vary from person to person, but on average, it lasts between 1 to 3 years. Several factors can influence how long the results last, including skin type, lifestyle, and exposure to the sun. Over time, the pigments will gradually fade, and the color may change slightly. To maintain the desired look, periodic touch-up sessions are recommended, typically every 12 to 18 months.

Aftercare

After your Semi-Permanent Eyebrow Microshading treatment at Skintastic, it’s important to follow some simple aftercare instructions:

Avoid Sun Exposure: Protect your newly enhanced brows from the sun’s harmful rays.

No Excessive Moisture: Avoid excessive moisture on the brow area for the first few days to allow the pigments to settle properly.

No Makeup: While your brows heal, it’s best to avoid applying makeup to the treated area.
