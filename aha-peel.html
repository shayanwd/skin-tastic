AHA Peel

at a Glance:

An AHA peel, or Alpha Hydroxy Acid peel, is a cosmetic procedure used to improve the texture and appearance of the skin

Procedure Time:

AHA peels are typically quick procedures that can be done in as little as 15 to 30 minutes. The exact time may vary depending on the specific type and concentration of AHA used and the areas being treated.

Number of Treatments: The number of AHA peel treatments required varies based on individual skin concerns and the desired results. However, a series of treatments is often recommended to achieve optimal results. Commonly, a series of 4 to 6 treatments spaced 2 to 4 weeks apart is recommended for noticeable improvements.

Duration of Results: The results of an AHA peel are not permanent, and the duration of results can vary from person to person. Typically, the benefits of an AHA peel can last for several months, with improvements in skin texture, tone, and reduction in fine lines and pigmentation. However, to maintain the results, periodic maintenance treatments may be necessary, often every 1 to 3 months, depending on individual skin needs.

Benefits

Enhanced Skin Texture: AHA peels help improve skin texture by reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Acne Reduction: If you struggle with acne, AHA peels can help unclog pores and reduce breakouts.

Even Skin Tone: Say goodbye to uneven skin tone and dark spots as AHA peels can help lighten pigmentation.

Youthful Glow: Experience a youthful glow as AHA peels stimulate collagen production, making your skin plumper and more radiant.

Procedure

Cleansing: Your skin will be thoroughly cleansed to remove any makeup and impurities.

AHA Application: The AHA solution will be applied to your skin, and you may feel a slight tingling sensation.

Neutralization: After a specific time, the solution will be neutralized, and your skin will be cleansed again.

Aftercare:

To maintain the fantastic results of your AHA peel, follow these tips:

Regular Skincare Routine: Establish a consistent skincare routine using products recommended by your skincare specialist.

Sun Protection: Continue wearing sunscreen daily to prevent sun damage.

Hydration: Keep your skin hydrated by drinking water and using a hydrating moisturizer.

FAQs

Q1: Is the AHA peel painful?

A1: The AHA peel may cause a mild tingling sensation, but it’s generally not painful.

Q2: How often should I get an AHA peel?

A2: The frequency depends on your skin type and goals, but many people get it every 4-6 weeks.

Q3: Can I wear makeup after an AHA peel?

A3: It’s best to avoid makeup for the first 24 hours after the peel to let your skin recover.

Q4: Are there any side effects of AHA peels?

A4: Possible side effects include redness, mild swelling, and sensitivity, but they usually subside within a few days.

Q5: Can AHA peels be combined with other treatments?

A5: Yes, your skincare specialist can customize a treatment plan that may include AHA peels alongside other skincare procedures for optimal results.
