FAQs

Q: What’s the difference between semi-permanent and permanent eyeliner?

A:Semi-permanent eyeliner fades over time and allows for style changes. Permanent eyeliner, as the name suggests, is permanent and less flexible in terms of style adjustments.

Q: Does the procedure hurt?

A: Most clients report minimal discomfort, often described as a slight scratching sensation. A numbing cream is applied before the procedure to minimize any discomfort.

Q: Can I choose the thickness and style of my eyeliner?

A: Absolutely! Skintastic offers a range of thicknesses and styles to suit your preferences and facial features.

Are there any side effects I should be aware of?

While rare, some clients may experience minor swelling or redness immediately after the procedure. These effects typically subside within a few hours to a day.

Can I remove semi-permanent eyeliner if I change my mind?

Yes, it’s possible to remove semi-permanent eyeliner, although it may require multiple sessions. Skintastic also offers removal services if you decide to change your look.
