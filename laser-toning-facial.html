at a Glance

Product Time: The Laser Tonic Facial procedure typically takes around 30 to 60 minutes to complete, making it a convenient option for those with busy schedules.

Number of Treatments: The number of treatments required for optimal results can vary depending on individual skin concerns and goals. While noticeable improvements can often be seen after a single session, a series of 3 to 6 treatments, spaced about 2 to 4 weeks apart, is generally recommended for more comprehensive and longer-lasting outcomes.

Duration of Results: The results of a Laser Tonic Facial are not only immediate but also continue to improve over time as collagen production is stimulated and cellular rejuvenation takes place. With proper post-treatment care and maintenance, the results can last for several months. To sustain the benefits, periodic maintenance treatments, usually once every 2 to 3 months, are advised.

Procedure

Consultation: Skintastic Professionals will assess your skin type, concerns, and goals to determine if the Laser Tonic Facial is suitable for you. Cleansing: The treatment begins with a thorough cleansing to remove makeup, dirt, and impurities from the skin’s surface.

Laser Application: A specially calibrated laser is used to target specific skin areas, stimulating collagen production and addressing various imperfections.

Aftercare

Avoid direct sun exposure and wear a broad-spectrum sunscreen.

Follow the skincare routine recommended by your provider.

Hydrate adequately to support skin recovery.

Avoid abrasive exfoliation for a few days post-treatment.
