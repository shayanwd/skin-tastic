BB Glow Facial

at a Glance

Product Time: A single BB Glow facial session usually takes around 60 to 90 minutes, depending on the specific steps involved and the practitioner’s pace.

Number of Treatments: For optimal results, a series of 3 to 5 treatments, spaced about 2 to 4 weeks apart, is recommended. The exact number of treatments can vary based on individual skin conditions and desired outcomes.

Duration of Results: The results of a BB Glow facial are semi-permanent. After the recommended initial series of treatments, the effects can last anywhere from 4 to 6 months. However, individual results may vary due to factors like skin type, skincare routine, and lifestyle choices.

Benefits

Flawless Complexion: Say goodbye to uneven skin tone, blemishes, and redness. The BB Glow treatment offers you a virtually flawless complexion, akin to wearing a lightweight foundation.

Radiant Glow: Achieve that coveted radiant glow that’s often associated with healthy, youthful skin. The treatment enhances your skin’s natural luminosity.

Minimal Downtime: Unlike more invasive treatments, the BB Glow Facial usually involves minimal downtime. You can go about your day with your newfound radiance.

Procedure

Cleansing: Your skin is thoroughly cleansed to prepare for the treatment.

Serum Application: The specialized serum, tailored to your skin’s needs, is gently micro needled into the skin’s surface.

Coverage and Glow: As the serum is absorbed, you’ll notice an immediate improvement in your skin’s appearance, with imperfections visibly minimized and a radiant glow emerging.

Aftercare

Hydration: Keep your skin hydrated by using a gentle moisturizer regularly.

Sun Protection: Shield your skin from the sun’s rays with a broad-spectrum sunscreen to prevent any potential pigmentation.

Avoid Intense Treatments: Steer clear of harsh skincare treatments for a few days post-BB Glow to allow your skin to fully benefit from the procedure.
