FAQs

Q1: Is laser facial hair removal painful?

A1: The discomfort is minimal, often likened to a quick pinch or snap. The procedure is well-tolerated by most individuals.

Q2: How many sessions will I need?

A2: The number of sessions varies depending on your hair and skin type. Generally, 6-8 sessions are recommended for optimal results.

Q3: Can I shave between sessions?

A3: Yes, shaving is encouraged between sessions, but avoid plucking or waxing to ensure the treatment’s effectiveness.

Q4: Will there be any downtime?

A4: There’s typically no downtime after the procedure. You can resume your daily activities immediately.

Q5: How long will the results last?

A5: Laser facial hair removal offers semi-permanent results. While some touch-up sessions might be needed over time, the treated hair won’t grow back as before.
