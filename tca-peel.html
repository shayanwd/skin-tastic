TCA Peel

at a Glance

Procedure Time: The time required for a TCA peel procedure can vary depending on the depth of the peel and the specific treatment plan. Generally, a TCA peel session can take anywhere from 15 minutes to an hour. Deeper TCA peels may take longer.

Number of Treatments: The number of TCA peel treatments needed depends on the individual’s skin condition and the desired level of improvement. Some people may see noticeable results after a single treatment, while others may require a series of treatments spaced several weeks apart for more significant results. Your dermatologist or skincare professional will recommend the appropriate number of sessions during a consultation.

Duration of Results: The results of a TCA peel can be long-lasting, often ranging from several months to a year or more. However, the duration of results can vary depending on factors such as the depth of the peel, individual skin type, and sun exposure. To maintain and extend the benefits of a TCA peel, it’s essential to follow a proper skincare routine and sun protection practices.

Procedure

The TCA Peel procedure at Skintastic is a carefully orchestrated process designed to rejuvenate your skin. Here’s a step-by-step overview:

Consultation: Your skincare specialist will assess your skin’s condition and discuss your desired outcomes.

Benefits

Opting for a TCA Peel offers a multitude of benefits, including:

Wrinkle Reduction: TCA Peels are effective in reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Improved Skin Texture: The peel helps to smooth out rough or uneven skin texture.

Sun Damage Reversal: It can fade sunspots and pigmentation irregularities.

Acne Scar Minimization: TCA Peels are renowned for diminishing the appearance of acne scars.

Youthful Glow: By stimulating collagen production, the peel leaves your skin looking youthful and radiant.

Aftercare

Following a TCA Peel at Skintastic, adhering to the recommended aftercare guidelines is essential. This may include:

Sun Protection: Shield your skin from the sun by applying sunscreen regularly.

Moisturizing: Keep your skin hydrated to support the healing process.

Avoiding Picking: Resist the urge to pick at any peeling skin.

Avoiding Harsh Products: Steer clear of abrasive skincare products for a few days post-peel.
